Hello

Learning English has never been easier!

We are GLOBAL LANGUAGE ACADEMY, a language school based in the sunshiny metro of Taguig, Philippines! We offer a fun and engaging English as a Second Language (ESL) program.

GLA's curriculum emphasizes the value of practical learning. Students experience a regimented and holistic approach to learning. Methodologies include practice through repetition, application through relevant projects and activities, and personal mentorship. Our goal is to give our students an edge that will help them thrive in a multicultural, multilingual, and diverse community through our specialized language programs.
